Venice



I visted Venice with my family when I was 15 and I can honestly say it is the most intriguing city I have ever visited. The unique landscape is complemented by a glamorous culture that really sums up the beauty of Italy. The fact that people travel from place to place on water and only water made me love this place and I would love to visit Venice again.
