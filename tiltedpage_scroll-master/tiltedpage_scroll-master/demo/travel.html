Travel

Venice

I am extremely lucky to have been able to travel around most of Europe during my childhood and am looking forward to continuing this trend when I am an adult. Out of all the cities I have experienced, Venice stood out the most to me with its unique, picturesque atmosphere and culture. The beautiful landscape of Venice had me amazed as I travelled around the city on a gondola.


Spain

I have been to Spain on numerous occasions. Pictured above is Badalona beach on the outskirts of Barcelona. Spain is by far my favourite country to visit. My love for Spain is aided by the fact that I studied Spanish in school which makes it far easier for me to enjoy my time in Spain. I would hopefully like to live there some day in the sun.
