Gaelic Football

From a very early age, I fell in love with Gaelic football. My father drilled into me from an early age what football meant to people from Kerry so I made it my goal to wear the county colours at some stage. I represented my county at underage level and recently witnessed my local club Beaufort lift an All Ireland title. I have been to Croke Park on numerous occasions witnessing Kerry capture gold and it is a sport that I will continue to follow for the rest of my life.

The Kerry GAA team who are preparing to dethrone Dublin in this years 2019 championship, they face an uphill task, however.

Soccer

I would describe myself as being somewhat obsessed with soccer. I have played the game all my life at club and county level, representing Killarney Celtic in the National Cup final before. I have a great interest in the tactics and fundamentals of the game and I aspire to be a coach in the foreseeable future. I am a diehard Liverpool FC fan having visited Anfield on numerous occasions. Liverpool play a big part in my life and they play a large role in determining my mood for the weekend.
